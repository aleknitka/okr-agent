## Strategic Context

We are a B2B SaaS company providing project management tooling to mid-market engineering teams. In Q3 2025 our priorities are reducing churn, expanding within existing accounts, and shipping the integrations roadmap that unblocks enterprise deals.

## Company Objectives

### CO-1: Reduce customer churn and grow net revenue retention

**Objective**: Keep the customers we have and grow revenue within them by making the product indispensable to their daily workflow.

**Key Results**
1. Increase net revenue retention from 88% to 95% by end of Q3
2. Reduce 90-day churn rate from 12% to 7%
3. Increase average seat expansion per account from 1.3x to 1.8x

---

### CO-2: Ship the integrations roadmap to unblock enterprise deals

**Objective**: Deliver the top three integrations required by our enterprise pipeline so that no deal is lost on product grounds.

**Key Results**
1. Ship Jira, GitHub, and Slack integrations to GA by week 8 of Q3
2. Achieve a CSAT score of ≥4.2/5 on integrations from beta customers
3. Convert at least 4 enterprise pipeline deals that cited missing integrations as a blocker

---

### CO-3: Improve onboarding so new customers reach value faster

**Objective**: Cut the time it takes a new team to get genuine value from the product, reducing early churn and support load.

**Key Results**
1. Reduce time-to-first-project from 4 days to 1 day for new signups
2. Increase 30-day activation rate (≥3 active users) from 54% to 70%
3. Reduce onboarding support tickets per new account from 6.2 to 2.5

---

## Business Context Notes

- Engineering capacity is constrained: two senior engineers are on the integrations track and unavailable for other product work until week 9
- Customer Success is running a save campaign in August — any team work that surfaces at-risk accounts early will have high leverage
- The annual company offsite is in week 6; expect reduced velocity that week
- Platform teams should not commit to major infrastructure changes that could cause downtime during the integrations GA window (weeks 6–8)
